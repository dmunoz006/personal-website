Selected work

Computational Discovery of SARS-CoV-2 Mpro Inhibitors

A computational screening study, presented at ABRCMS 2021, that searched for small molecules capable of inhibiting the SARS-CoV-2 main protease (Mpro). As an LSAMP research assistant I ran quantitative and qualitative analysis on candidate compounds and summarized the results in tables, figures, and written reports.
